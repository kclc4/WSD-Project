The History of the Fender Stratocaster

The very first Fender Stratocaster was produced in 1954 and it has remained one of the most popular guitars of all time. Guitarists such as Eric Clapton, Jeff Beck, Jimi Hendrix, Jack Bruce and Ginger Baker have used the Stratocaster in their careers.
