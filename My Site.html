    • About

      The VTX series began in 2002 with the larger version being the VTX-1800. Honda realized the bike was too large for many riders, so they followed it up by the smaller VTX-1300. The VTX-1300 was a more manageable size with no shortage of power. It soon began to sell better than its larger version.

      The VTX-1300 is a liquid-cooled 1312cc V-twin engine. The engine hangs on mounts that are designed to absorb most of the motor's rumble. This gives the rider a more comfortable and enjoyable ride.
